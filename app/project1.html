Case Study #1

Integrating Prototyping into the Company workflow

Problem

The old development workflow was a word doc to code. The CEO would instruct the developers on edits after something was coded up and it was not time effective.

Tools

Tasks & Responsibilities

Using Adobe XD I reverse engineered the companies websites and assets from code to artboards.

The artboards were wired up to simulate the live sites.

XD was used to create development specs to hand off to the developer.

Results

Moving from a "develop first, fix later" strategy, to leveraging UX/UI software allowed the company to iterate and create design solutions faster, discover edge cases earlier and avoid costly redesigns all together.
